August - November 2025

Job Search Website

At Texas Tech University, for Intro to Engineering Design for Computer Science Majors (ENGR-1340), we were tasked with interviewing engineering students and identifying 3 pain points with current job search solutions (e.g. Indeed.com). Using the Business Model Canvas and Lean Launchpad, we currated a design intended to relieve these pain points while maintaining a feasable business model. Using HTML and CSS we developed our first website. We used GitHub to collaborate and track changes. We presented our design process to the professor, then recorded our final presentation with the completed website and submitted it to the dean for judging in an Innovation Hub competition.
